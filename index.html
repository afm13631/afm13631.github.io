Manchester United

Manchester United is one of the most decorated football clubs in the world. The club is found in Manchester, England and their home stadium is called Old Trafford. They play in the top English division called the English Premier League. United has won more trophies than any other English football club and in 2108 Manchester United was named the most valuable football club in the world.

Manchester United is known to have big named, world-class players throughout its history like Cristiano Ronaldo, a few years back, and some who are currently on the squad now including Paul Pogba.

Some legendary players include:

  • George Best
  • Sir Bobby Charlton
  • Eric Cantona
  • Roy Keane

During the long season, 3 points are awarded for a win, 1 point for a draw and 0 points for a loss. The club with the most points at the end of the season is crowned English Premier League Champions. As the current season is coming to an end, United sits in 5th place but only 2 points behind Chelsea who is in 4th place. The top 4 teams qualify for The Champions League. The Champions League takes all of the top teams from Europe and puts them all in a massive tournament to see who the best in the world is.
